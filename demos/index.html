Spatial Web

Depth for the DOM, not for a canvas.

`spatial-web` is a small library for semantic z-axis layout, physical shadows, depth hover, and z-scroll on regular HTML.

Use it when you want layered cards, dashboard hierarchy, scrollytelling, or 3D-feeling UI without jumping to WebGL or Three.js.

Why use it

Most interfaces fake depth with custom transforms, arbitrary shadows, and isolated hover logic. `spatial-web` gives those pieces one consistent scene model.

Good fit for

feature cards, layered dashboards, inspector panels, narrative scroll sections, and experimental UI generated by humans or AI coding tools.

Not for

meshes, materials, cameras, game rendering, or full scene graphs that belong in WebGL.
